The Shamir's Secret Sharing Algorithm

The Shamir's Secret Sharing Algorithm is a clever method to divide a secret into multiple parts (shares), ensuring that only a sufficient number of parts can reconstruct the secret. This algorithm enhances security by distributing the secret among multiple guardians.

Here’s how you can use this website to encode and decode your secret:

How to Encode Your Secret

  1. Enter the numerical value of your secret in the "Encoder" section.
  2. Specify Shares and Threshold: Indicate the total number of shares you want to create and set the threshold. The threshold is the minimum number of shares required to reconstruct the secret.
  3. Generate Shares: Click on the "Generate Shares" button. The secret will be split into the specified number of shares, each associated with a guardian. Each share will be displayed along with the name of its guardian and an image.
  4. To decode the secret, input the shares in the "Decoder" section and click "Reconstruct Secret".

How to Decode Your Secret

  1. Collect Shares: Gather at least the number of shares specified by the threshold. These shares can come from different guardians, but you must have at least the threshold number to reconstruct the secret.
  2. Input Shares: In the "Decoder" section, enter each share on a new line in the provided textarea. Ensure that each share is formatted correctly, for example:
    Hannibal: (x: 3, y: 16480330010)
    Alan Turing: (x: 4, y: 14411271815)
    Sherlock Holmes: (x: 5, y: 71619513970)
    Wonder Woman: (x: 6, y: 194838334)
  3. Reconstruct the Secret: Click on the "Reconstruct Secret" button. The algorithm will use the entered shares to reconstruct and display the original secret.
